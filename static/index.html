Classification Rules

HIGH Risk

Location is inside a water body or forest zone. Construction is not permitted — violates environmental protection laws.

MEDIUM Risk

Location is within 100 meters of a sensitive zone. May require environmental clearance before development.

LOW Risk

Location is at a safe distance (>100m) from all zones. Standard permits apply.
